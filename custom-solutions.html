Case Studies

Examples of custom solutions we've developed for our clients:

Precision Oncology Platform

We developed a comprehensive genomic analysis platform for a leading cancer center to guide personalized treatment decisions. The solution integrated somatic mutation profiling, gene expression analysis, and clinical data to identify actionable therapeutic targets.

The platform included custom algorithms for variant prioritization, a knowledge base of clinically relevant genomic alterations, and an intuitive physician-facing interface to facilitate interpretation of complex genomic data.

Results

  • Reduced analysis time from 2 weeks to 3 days
  • Increased identification of actionable mutations by 35%
  • Guided treatment decisions for over 5,000 patients
  • Enabled clinical trial matching for rare tumor types

Clinical Trial Biomarker Analysis Pipeline

For a pharmaceutical company conducting a Phase II clinical trial, we developed an integrated biomarker analysis pipeline to identify patient subgroups most likely to respond to a novel targeted therapy.

The system processed and analyzed multiple data types (genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic) to identify molecular signatures associated with treatment response, enabling more informed decisions for the subsequent Phase III trial design.

Results

  • Identified a novel 15-gene signature predictive of response
  • Enabled patient stratification that increased response rate from 23% to 68%
  • Expedited regulatory approval process
  • Provided basis for companion diagnostic development
